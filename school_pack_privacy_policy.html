📱 About School Pack
School Pack is a comprehensive school management application designed for educational institutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and have built our app with a strict no-data-collection policy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to School Pack ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Important Note: School Pack is designed for use by educational institutions and their authorized personnel. We prioritize user privacy and have built our app with a strict no-data-collection policy.

2. Information We Collect

School Pack does NOT collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. Our application operates on a local-first architecture where:

  • All data entered by users (student records, marks, reports, etc.) is stored directly on the user's device
  • No information is transmitted to external servers except for authentication and synchronization with the school's own database
  • We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or third-party tracking tools
  • We do not collect device information, location data, or usage statistics
  • No personal information is shared with third parties

3. How School Pack Uses Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. The information you enter into School Pack is used solely for:

  • Managing school operations within the app interface
  • Generating report cards, marksheets, and other academic reports
  • Synchronizing with the school's own database (if configured by the institution)
  • Providing offline access to school data

4. Data Storage and Security

All data entered into School Pack is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local data is encrypted using your device's native security features
  • Authentication credentials are stored securely using encrypted shared preferences
  • No data is transmitted to external servers without explicit user action
  • Users can clear all local data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

5. Third-Party Services

School Pack does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external connections are:

  • Authentication: When you log in, credentials are verified against your school's server
  • Data Synchronization: When explicitly requested, data can be synchronized with your school's database
  • API Calls: All API calls are made directly to your school's server, not through our infrastructure
  • WebView Integration: Report generation and settings pages use WebView to display school-hosted content

6. Camera and Gallery Access

School Pack may request access to your device's camera and gallery for the following purposes:

  • QR Code Scanning: Scanning QR codes on report cards for verification purposes
  • Student Photos: Uploading student profile pictures
  • Staff Photos: Uploading staff member photographs
  • School Logo: Uploading school badge/logo during enrollment

All images are stored locally on your device and are only transmitted to your school's server when you explicitly choose to upload them. School Pack does not access or store these images on our servers.

Note: You can deny camera/gallery permissions and School Pack will still function for core features.

7. Children's Privacy

School Pack is intended for use by school administrators, teachers, and staff. The app may contain information about children (students), but this information is:

  • Entered and managed by authorized school personnel only
  • Stored locally on the authorized user's device
  • Only transmitted to the school's own database (not to School Pack servers)
  • Not collected, stored, or processed by School Pack

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Since School Pack does not collect or store your personal information, you have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete any data from the app at any time
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling School Pack removes all locally stored data
  • Permissions: You can refuse to grant camera/gallery permissions
  • School Data: Contact your school administrator for data stored on school servers
  • Export Data: School Pack does not provide data export as all data is already on your device

9. Data Retention

School Pack does not retain any user data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device and remains until you:

  • Manually delete the data within the app
  • Clear app data from device settings
  • Uninstall the application

Data stored on your school's server is subject to your school's data retention policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of School Pack after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Data Deletion Request

As School Pack does not store any user data on our servers, there is no data for us to delete. To delete your data:

  • Local Device Data: Go to Settings → Apps → School Pack → Clear Data, or simply uninstall the app
  • School Server Data: Contact your school administrator to request data deletion from the school's database

For any data deletion assistance, please email us at support@schoolpack.com

14. Security Measures

School Pack implements the following security measures:

  • Encrypted local storage using Flutter Secure Storage
  • Secure authentication using session management
  • No hardcoded credentials or API keys
  • All network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
