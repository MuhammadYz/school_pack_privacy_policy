📱 About School Pack
School Pack is a comprehensive cloud-based school management application designed for educational institutions. We use secure cloud services to store and manage your school data, ensuring accessibility, reliability, and security.

1. Introduction

Welcome to School Pack ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and cloud services.

Important Note: School Pack is a cloud-based school management system. Your data is stored on our secure cloud servers to enable multi-device access, real-time synchronization, and data backup.

2. Information We Collect

School Pack collects the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

School Information:

  • School name, address, and contact details
  • School logo and branding materials
  • Curriculum type (Secular/Theology)
  • Study levels offered (Nursery, Primary, Secondary)

Student Information:

  • Student names, registration numbers, and photos
  • Academic records, marks, and grades
  • Attendance records
  • Medical information (as provided by parents/guardians)
  • Parent/guardian contact information

Staff Information:

  • Staff names, employee IDs, and photos
  • Contact information and professional details
  • Teaching assignments and subject allocations
  • Signatures for report cards

User Account Information:

  • Username and hashed password
  • User role and permissions (Admin, Teacher, etc.)
  • Login activity and session data

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • IP addresses for security and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

School Pack uses the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide school management features including student registration, marks entry, report generation, and class management
  • Authentication: To verify user identity and control access to school data
  • Synchronization: To enable seamless access across multiple devices
  • Backup and Recovery: To prevent data loss through automated backups
  • Report Generation: To create report cards, performance lists, and other academic reports
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications (when enabled)

4. Cloud Storage and Security

School Pack uses enterprise-grade secure cloud services to store your data:

  • Data Centers: We use reputable cloud service providers with data centers located in secure facilities
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized users can access specific data
  • Regular Backups: Automated daily backups to prevent data loss
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for unauthorized access attempts
🔒 SSL/TLS Encryption 🔐 AES-256 Encryption 💾 Daily Backups 🛡️ 24/7 Monitoring

5. Third-Party Service Providers

School Pack uses trusted third-party services to support our operations:

  • Cloud Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure providers for data storage
  • Database Services: Managed database services for reliable data storage
  • CDN Services: Content delivery networks for optimal performance

All third-party providers are carefully vetted and contractually obligated to maintain data security and confidentiality.

6. Camera and Gallery Access

School Pack may request access to your device's camera and gallery for the following purposes:

  • QR Code Scanning: Scanning QR codes on report cards for verification
  • Student Photos: Uploading student profile pictures
  • Staff Photos: Uploading staff member photographs
  • School Logo: Uploading school badge/logo

All uploaded images are securely stored on our cloud servers. You can deny these permissions, though some features may be limited.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

School Pack does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operating our service (under confidentiality agreements)
  • School Administration: Within your school's authorized personnel based on role permissions

8. Children's Privacy

School Pack is intended for use by school administrators, teachers, and staff. The app contains information about children (students), which is:

  • Collected and managed by authorized school personnel
  • Stored securely on our cloud servers
  • Used only for legitimate educational purposes
  • Protected by strict access controls

Parents or guardians who wish to review or request deletion of their child's information should contact their child's school directly.

9. Data Retention

School Pack retains your data for as long as your school maintains an active account:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your school subscription is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data is retained for 90 days after subscription ends, then securely deleted
  • Backups: Archived backups are retained for up to 30 days

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Export: Request export of your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact your school administrator or email us at privacy@schoolpack.com

11. International Data Transfer

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Cookies and Tracking

School Pack uses essential cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Performance and analytics (anonymized)
  • Preferences and settings storage

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new effective date
  • Displaying a notice within the app
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

14. Security Incidents and Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
  • Provide details of the incident and affected data
  • Recommend steps to protect affected information
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
